Events and Festivals

Discover the vibrant and colorful events and festivals celebrated in Liloan, Cebu.

San Fernando Rey Feast Day

Held every May, this cherished religious celebration honors the town's patron saint, San Fernando Rey.

Rosquillos Festival

Celebrated every May, the Rosquillos Festival pays homage to Liloan’s famous delicacy.

Christmas Festival of Lights

Every December, Liloan transforms into a dazzling wonderland during the Christmas Festival.

Barangay Fiesta Celebrations

Throughout the year, Liloan’s barangays host their own unique fiesta celebrations.
